Tuglife

An Interactive Museum Exhibit

The Project

Tuglife was a group project between myself and 5 colleagues at the Centre for Digital Media. Our objective was to build a prototype for an interactive exhibit at the Vancouver Maritime Museum. The goal of the exhibit was to educate guests of all ages on the day-to-day operations of Tugboats in Vancouver harbour and why they are essential. My primary role in the team was programming the physics simulation using C# in Unity. Unfortunately, since this work is proprietary the code and project documentation cannot be shared.
